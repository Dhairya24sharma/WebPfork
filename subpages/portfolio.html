Projects Portfolio

1. Railway Management

  • My first project
  • Objective:The Railway Management System is designed to simplify the process of railway ticket booking and provide real-time train tracking status. The goal is to create a seamless connection between passengers and train companies, ensuring a fast and efficient system.
  • Features:
    • Easy railway ticket booking.
    • Real-time train tracking status.
    • Improved communication between passengers and train companies.
  • Technologies Used:Python for backend development and MySQL for database management.

2. PawSome Adoptions

  • Objective:PawSome Adoptions, developed during a hackathon, aims to facilitate the adoption of stray animals. It serves as a connection portal between rescuers and potential adopters, ensuring a secure system for transferring personal details between the two parties.
  • Features:
    • Connection portal for rescuers and adopters.
    • Secure transfer of personal details.
  • Technologies Used:HTML, CSS (Tailwind), and Vanilla JavaScript for frontend development.


3. VitAmart

  • Objective:Created during a hackathon, Vitamart focuses on establishing a platform for product trading within VIT and other engineering colleges. The project includes a secure chatting system between buyers and sellers, enabling effective communication about products, along with an auction process to prevent unfair pricing.
  • Features:
    • Secure chatting system for buyer-seller communication.
    • Auction process for fair pricing.
  • Technologies Used:SVELTE Framework for frontend development.
